UX Writing

During my 6 years working in QA, designs with inefficient or confusing wording had me considering the user well before entering the UX world. This is how we communicate with our end users, so we must do so effectively.

Accessibility

As a neurodivergent designer, accessibility concerns will always take a front-seat when prioritizing design tasks and issues. When we design universally, we design for everybody, so it should be a top priority.

Web Development
