About Me

My name is Rebecca Van Aken and I'm from the greater Philadelphia area.

I graduated from Elizabethtown College in 2017 with a degree in Early Childhood and Special Education. For the past 5+ years, I've been a QA Engineer at a medical software company. I worked on a variety of projects from lab instruments to mobile apps and I've developed a lot of skills that would benefit me in many areas of the tech industry.

In April, 2022 I graduated from a 6 month UX/UI bootcamp from Penn LPS. You can see more about some of the projects I did for that in my portfolio.

In my free time, I like to read, write, play games with my family. My favorite ice cream flavor is Mint Chocolate Chip and my favorite place to vacation is the Adirondack Mountains.
